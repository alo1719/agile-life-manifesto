# 敏捷生活宣言

## Manifesto for Agile Living Style

1. 我们最高的目标，是持续不断地尽早做有价值的事。

    Our ultimate goal is to continuously do valuable things as early as possible.

2. 激发自己的斗志，给自己创造需要的环境，并信任自己和所干的事的价值。

    Motivate ourselves, create the environment we need, and have faith in both ourselves and the value of what we do.

3. 以终为始，但相比于遵循计划更注重适应变化。

    Begin with the end in mind, but focus more on adapting to changes than following a plan.

4. 欣然面对生活中或好或坏的变化，即使在低谷期也一样。毕竟，影响我们生活的更多的是顺利的而不是那些出问题的地方。

    Embrace changes in life, whether good or bad, even during tough times. After all, it’s the smooth parts that affect our lives more than the problematic ones.

5. 以简洁为本，它是极力减少不必要工作的艺术。

    Simplicity is essential; it is the art of minimizing unnecessary work.

6. 敏捷生活倡导可持续生活。敏捷生活者应该能够长期保持稳定的生活节奏。

    Agile living promotes a sustainable lifestyle. An agile person should be able to maintain a consistent and stable pace of life over the long term.

7. 不断地关注优秀的个体和技能能够增强敏捷生活的能力。

    Continuous attention to excellent individuals and skills enhances agility.

8. 经常性地反思如何能够优化生活，并依此调整自身的行为习惯。倾向于采取较短的周期。

    Frequently reflect on how to optimize life and adjust habits accordingly, preferably in shorter cycles.

9. 面对面的交谈是最富有效果和效率的信息传递方式，也更有利于构建人际关系。

    Face-to-face conversation is the most efficient and effective way of conveying information and is also more conducive to building relationships.

10. 无论何时，记住自己是被爱的。爱那些同样爱自己的人。

    Always remember that you are loved. Love those who love you.
