# 睡眠宣言

## Manifesto for Sleep

1. 我们睡眠的目标，是为了休整和放松，提高睡眠后的表现。

   Our goal in sleep is to rest and relax, and to improve our performance after sleep.

2. 控制睡眠环境，尽量减少光、声音，保持合适的温度。可以利用眼罩、耳塞等工具辅助。

   Control the sleep environment, reduce light and sound as much as possible, and keep the temperature appropriate. Use eye masks, earplugs etc. to assist.

3. 设置固定时间的闹钟，把闹钟放到自己需要起床才能关闭的地方。

   Set a fixed time alarm clock, and put the alarm clock in a place where you need to get up to turn it off.

4. 不把带有屏幕的数码产品带上床。

   Do not bring digital products with screens to bed.

5. 别把睡眠质量看得太重，一晚上没睡好不会毁了你的生活。在有压力时使用深呼吸放松。

   Do not take sleep quality too seriously. A bad night's sleep will not ruin your life. Use deep breathing to relax when under pressure.
